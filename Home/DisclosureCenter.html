FIN Diclosure Center

FIN Disclosure features desktop and mobile tools for firms to store and deliver disclosures to clients, staff, solicitors, and prospects.

Our desktop includes portals to send disclosures via a web link. The mobile app, integrated with our desktop solution, allows firms to elect to deliver disclosure documents via a smartphone to enhance its customer experience.

Desktop & Mobile Application

  • Deliver Disclosures for:
  • Continuity plans
  • Privacy Policies
  • Form ADV, CRS and advertising notices.
  • Marketing Material

  • Capture Reports for:
  • Access Person logs
  • Code of Ethics
  • Other Business Activities
  • Advertising
  • Personal Securities Transactions
